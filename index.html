ABOUT

Hi, I’m Padma Christie — an English teacher passionate about helping students build confidence in using English.

With experience teaching in Taiwan, I specialize in working with elementary and middle school students (國小、國中), guiding them to improve their language skills through engaging and interactive lessons.

I believe that learning English should be both fun and meaningful. In my classes, I use competitive group games, teamwork activities, and interactive exercises to keep students engaged while helping them develop essential communication skills. My goal is to create a supportive environment where students feel encouraged to express themselves in English without fear.

With patience, creativity, and a well-tempered approach, I adapt my teaching to different learning styles and classroom dynamics. I’m fluent in English, Mandarin Chinese, Cantonese, Hokkien, and Malay, which allows me to bridge language gaps and communicate clearly with both students and parents.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy playing the violin, playing guitar, singing, and exploring nature. These passions influence my teaching, as I believe music and nature can inspire creativity and learning.

I am currently looking for a teaching position in southern Taiwan where I can bring my energy and experience to help students succeed. If you’re looking for a teacher who is patient, compassionate, and skilled at making English fun, I’d love to connect!
